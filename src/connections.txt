Connections from STM32f746g disco to L289N
PA8 (Pin D7) - ENA (PWM speed control)
PB4 (Pin D5) - IN1 (Direction control)
PB5 (Pin D4) - IN2 (Direction control)
GND - GND (Common Ground)

Driver to Motor/Power:

Driver Output 1 & 2 - Motor Terminals
External Battery (+) - Driver VCC (12V/5V)
External Battery (-) - Driver GND
